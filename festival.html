Tunisian Soul

A celebration of sound, spirit, and ancient desert rhythms.

International Festivals

Symphonic El Jem

Experience the grandeur of classical music inside the world’s best-preserved Roman amphitheater. Under the soft glow of thousands of hand-lit candles, international orchestras perform timeless masterpieces. It is a majestic encounter where Roman history meets the elegance of symphonic art.

Sahara Festival

Celebrated every December, this festival is a grand tribute to the nomadic Bedouin spirit and desert heritage. Witness the thrilling "Fantasia" horse races, traditional camel marathons, and the soul-stirring poetry of the dunes. It is an authentic immersion into the wild heart of the Tunisian Sahara.

Oasis Festival

Located in the lush palm groves of Tozeur, this festival celebrates the annual date harvest and Saharan life. The city comes alive with traditional folk music, street performances, and desert storytelling amidst the stunning brick architecture. It is a vibrant display of life, color, and southern hospitality.

Mystical Music & Trance

El Hadhra

El Hadhra is a powerful Sufi performance that blends deep rhythmic drumming with spiritual chanting. It creates a hypnotic atmosphere of peace and collective trance that resonates through the ancient Medinas. This performance is a celebrated masterpiece of Tunisian spiritual art, bringing together hundreds of performers on one stage.

The Malouf

The Malouf is the refined, classical music of Tunisia, inherited from our ancient Andalusian roots. Performed with ouds, violins, and poetic verses, it represents the sophisticated soul of the Mediterranean coast. It is the soundtrack of Tunisian celebrations, symbolizing elegance, history, and artistic perfection.

The Stambali

Stambali is a mystical ritual music that blends Sub-Saharan rhythms with Tunisian spiritual traditions. Using the deep-toned Goumbri and metallic Chqachqas, it takes the audience on a journey of rhythmic healing and trance. It is a living testament to Tunisia’s diverse identity and its historic connection to the heart of Africa.
