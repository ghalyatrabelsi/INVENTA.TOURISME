Global Leader

A Sea of Olive Trees

Tunisia is home to nearly 100 million olive trees, stretching from the northern hills to the southern Saharan edges. We proudly stand as the second-largest exporter of olive oil in the world, supplying the finest kitchens from Europe to America with our organic essence.

Did you know? Some Tunisian olive trees are over 2,000 years old and still producing!
Taste & Wellness

The Flavor of Purity

What makes Tunisian olive oil unique is its high polyphenol content and its exceptional, sunny flavor profile. Whether it's the fruity and intense Chetoui or the sweet and mild Chemlali, our oil is a powerhouse of antioxidants and a pillar of the healthy Mediterranean diet.

Our oils consistently win gold medals in international competitions in London, New York, and Tokyo for their purity and unmatched quality.

The Gourmet Experience

Discover the Terroir: Chetoui vs. Chemlali

Much like fine wine, Tunisian olive oil is defined by its 'Terroir.' In our private tasting sessions, you will learn to distinguish between our two legendary varieties:

  • Chetoui (The North): Intense, robust, and peppery with notes of green grass—perfect for bold dishes.
  • Chemlali (The South/Sahel): Mild, fruity, and buttery with a hint of almond—ideal for delicate finishing.
Tunisian oils consistently sweep Gold Medals in New York and Tokyo for their unmatched chemical purity.
Health & Beauty

Liquid Gold for the Body & Soul

In Tunisia, olive oil is our oldest secret for longevity and beauty. High in polyphenols and antioxidants, our organic oil is a powerhouse for the heart. Beyond the kitchen, it has been used for centuries by Tunisian women as a natural elixir for glowing skin and radiant hair.

Experience an Olive-Oil Spa Ritual or bring home our artisanal olive-oil soaps, infused with jasmine and rosemary.
